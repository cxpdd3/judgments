Investinet

InvestiNet is a full service account receivables management firm. They perform investigations and have a legal enforcement network to assist their clients in determining the value of their inventories. Their services include Non-Performing Judgments, Legal Collections, and Real Estate Servicing.

Resurgent Capital Services

Resurgent Capital Services is a financial services company. They manage and service domestic and international consumer debt portfolios for credit grantors and debt buyers. They manage accounts across the credit spectrum including performing accounts, sub- and non-performing accounts, secured accounts, and unsecured accounts.
